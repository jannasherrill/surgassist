An essential tool in the operating room

SurgAssist represents the latest advancement in surgical software, aiming to simplify the tasks of all those present in the operating room. Our goal is to gather the preferences, supplies, and surgical setups of doctors during surgical procedures, consolidating them into a single platform. This effort supports allied health professionals in enhancing the fluidity and efficiency of surgeries, ultimately ensuring patients experience a seamless and secure journey.

Who We Are

SurgAssist aligns everyone that belongs in an operating room to eliminate any confusion or mistakes. Doctors are able to freely edit their own preference card at any time, it enables nurses to access specific preferences prior to surgeries, surgical technologists are confidently able to set up to the surgeon’s liking, and equipment technologists are able to view the settings chosen for each specific surgery and competently operate equipment as necessary.
